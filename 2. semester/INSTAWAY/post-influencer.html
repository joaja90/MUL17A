Lokations baseret marketing på Instagram

One thing people always seem to forget is the fact that social networks really are, well.. social. There are billions of other people populating them, and a huge number of users embrace location tagging on Instagram. This provides an interesting opportunity for smart marketers and andividuals who are prepared to do some research on the people and organisations they want to be associated with - the ability to make sure they'll know who you are before you even get in touch. It really is simple. By interacting with people who post from specific locations, you can build an audience in those locations. Whether that's a business you want to work with, or a really elite club you want to join, if you work to find the specific people in those locations, and get them to follow you, then you can build your image
